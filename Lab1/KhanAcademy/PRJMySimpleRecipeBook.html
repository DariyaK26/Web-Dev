Spagetti Carbonara

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
spaghetti 8 ounces
large eggs 2
freshly grated Parmesan ½ cup
bacon, diced 4 slices
garlic, minced 4 cloves
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
chopped fresh parsley leaves 2 tablespoons

Step 1: In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta according to package instructions; reserve 1/2 cup water and drain well.

Step 2: In a small bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan; set aside.

Step 3: Heat a large cast iron skillet over medium high heat. Add bacon and cook until brown and crispy, about 6-8 minutes; reserve excess fat.

Step 4: Stir in garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute. Reduce heat to low.

Step 5: Working quickly, stir in pasta and egg mixture, and gently toss to combine; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Add reserved pasta water, one tablespoon at a time, until desired consistency is reached.

Step 6: Serve immediately, garnished with parsley, if desired.

Beef & Bean Burritos

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
ground beef 455 g
taco seasoning 2 tablespoons
tortillas 6 flour
refried bean 1 can(455 g)
shredded mexican cheese blend 100 g

Step 1: Add ground beef to a large skillet over high heat and sprinkle with taco seasoning. Cook, breaking up the meat, until browned. Drain the fat and set aside to cool.

Step 2: Assemble the burritos by microwaving the flour tortilla for 20 seconds, and begin to layer burrito starting with refried beans, followed by the cooked beef and cheese.

Step 3: Fold in the left and right sides of the tortilla and roll it up from the bottom, tucking the bottom edge under the filling. Wrap in parchment paper and label. Freeze up to 1 month.

Step 4: To reheat from frozen, wrap the burrito in a damp paper towel and microwave for 2-3 minutes, flipping halfway or until the center is hot. Let stand 1 minute before eating.

Source: Tasty/beef-bean-burritos.com

Dough Pizza

  • Time: 35 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
self-rising flour 1¾ cups(220 g)
greek yogurt 1 cup(245 g)
pizza sauce ½ cup(130 g)
shredded mozzarella cheese 1 cup(100 g)
part-skim pepperoni slice for topping

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).

Step 2: In a large bowl, mix the self-rising flour and Greek yogurt until it comes together to form a ball.

Step 3: Transfer the dough ball to a lightly floured work surface and use your hands to begin flattening and shaping the rough into about a 12-inch (30 cm) round. If at any point the dough begins to stick, sprinkle on a bit more flour.

Step 4: Carefully transfer the dough to a lightly floured baking sheet.

Step 5: Spread the sauce evenly across the dough, sprinkle on the cheese, and top with pepperonis.

Step 6: Bake for 20 minutes, until the cheese has melted and the crust has turned golden-brown.

Step 7: Slice and serve.
